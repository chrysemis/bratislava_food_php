I come from Bratislava - the capital of Slovakia which is on the border with Austria and very near to Hungary and Czech Republic. Historically, it used to be a very cosmopolitan city and its traditional cuisine is a mix of Slovak, Czech, Hungarian, Austrian, German, Croatian, Turkish and other food.
Today, I'm an expat living in England. I love cooking and trying new meals and the possibilities seem endless here! However, sometimes I like to get back to my roots and meals I grew up on - and to share them with people who like to try new ideas.
